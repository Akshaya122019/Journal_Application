Aims & Scope

This journal is an international forum dedicated to the advancement and application of soft computing techniques for addressing complex real-world problems. It promotes an integrated and interdisciplinary perspective that brings together diverse methodologies within the soft computing paradigm.

Soft computing comprises a collection of computational approaches that leverage tolerance for imprecision, uncertainty, and partial truth to achieve robust, adaptable, and cost-effective solutions. The journal emphasizes high-quality research that explores both theoretical developments and practical implementations of intelligent systems.

The scope includes original contributions in the application, advancement, and convergence of IOT & Smart System, Fuzzy, Decision Supporting and National Language Processing techniques. Particular attention is given to innovative solutions that demonstrate real-world relevance across engineering, science, industry, and emerging technologies.

The journal follows a continuous (rolling) publication model, enabling rapid online publication of articles immediately after final acceptance. This approach ensures timely dissemination of research and frequent updates with newly accepted work.

Major Topics:

The scope of this journal covers the following soft computing and related techniques, interactions between several soft computing techniques, and their industrial applications:

  • Machine and Deep Learning

  • Fuzzy Computing

  • oT &Smart Systems
  • Pattern Recognition
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Smart Cities & IoT
  • Supply Chain & Logistics
  • Agricultural Robotics
  • Human-Machine Interface
  • Software Engineering
  • Signal or Image Processing

Authors are welcome to submit letters promoting original soft computing research to Applied Soft Computing's open access companion title, Systems and Soft Computing.
