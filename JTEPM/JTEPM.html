Aims & Scope

Journal of Transportation Engineering, Planning, and Management is an international platform for all those engaged in inventing a better future for mobility. Unlike most technical journals, our publication aims to fill the gap between what academics have to offer, and what matters in practical applications. We aim to feature all those who deal with what matters most: from conceptualizing transportation infrastructure and beyond, to ensuring safe and sustainable road infrastructure. Issues like optimizing city streets or transportation logic for regions belong to this category. We aim to publish quality work which has the potential to induce change in practical implementations in transportation infrastructure.

With a practical application orientation, the scope of Journal of Transportation Engineering, Planning, and Management is mainly focused on the design and planning, as well as the operation and management of contemporary transport systems. It mainly deals with research that improves safety and efficiency and also aims at sustainability and reliability.

Journal of Transportation Engineering, Planning, and Management focuses on the intersection Traffic Engineering and Transportation Systems, Highway and Pavement Engineering and Transportation Planning and Policy. The scope of the journal includes, but is not limited to:

Major Topics:

  • Intelligent Transportation Systems
  • Public Transportation and Urban Mobility
  • Logistics and Freight Transportation
  • Transportation Safety and Risk Analysis
  • Sustainable and Green Transportation
  • Data Analytics and Optimization in Transportation

Authors are invited to submit original research articles, comprehensive reviews, and innovative methodologies that contribute significantly to the advancement of the field.
